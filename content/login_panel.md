## What MarketLens offers

**MarketLens** turns customer reviews and market signals into **competitive intelligence** you can act on.

- **Multi-industry coverage** — Compare leaders across dimensions that matter for each sector.
- **Audience-aware views** — Investors, companies, and customers each see charts tuned to how they decide.
- **AI insights** — Strategic takeaways from your data, not generic platitudes.

### Disclaimer

*This tool is for research and prototyping only. Outputs are not financial advice. Verify all figures against primary sources before making investment or business decisions.*
